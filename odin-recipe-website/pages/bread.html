Dutch Oven Bread

HOME ABOUT

Ingredient

  • 2 Cups Warm Water
  • 2 1/4 tsp Dry Instant Yeast
  • 4 cups Bread Flour
  • 1 1/4 tsp Salt

Directions

  1. Stir in Dry Instant Yeast to 2 cups of warm (100 F to 120 F) Water and let sit until foamy.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine 4 cups of Bread Flour and 1 1/4 tsp of Salt. Once thoroughly combined add foamy yeast mixture. Mix until a dough forms.
  3. Form dough into a generaly ball shape and let rest for 2 hours or until doubled in size. Fold the dough several times and let set 2 more hours or until doubled again.
  4. After second rise, preheat the oven to 450 F with the empty dutch oven. Form the dough into its final shape and let rise for 1 hour in a grease bowl while dutch oven heats up.
  5. Place dough into hot dutch oven and let cook, with the lid on for 30 minutes. Then remove the lid and let cook for another 15 minutes.
  6. Remove from the oven and let cool for 10 minutes.
  7. Enjoy!
